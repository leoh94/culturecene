Hip-hop History 101: The Golden Age

Welcome to Hip-hop. Here we will explore the golden era of hip-hop spanning between the mid-1980's to the early 1990's. The sounds, vibe and feel of the golden age tracks are representative of those years, through the technology available at the time, the cultural and societal norms regarding things like attitudes and for the most part, accelerated a much-needed exposure of the black struggles in America on to an international stage.
