Bass, Grills & Bounce

The south. Home of the Blues, Jazz and Rock'n'Roll. Host of the melting pot of Gumbo, soul food and barbequeue, black culture and history permeates the air in the south. So, what has the south done for hip-hop? Look around. The sound, attitude, slang and general nomenclature of hip-hop and the current mainstream state of rap have directly "bitten" from the fruit that the south has been offering since the 90's.
